Standalone file for testing purposes to distinguish multi-clump models in the data. Total flux is scaled to 3 x 10-4 ph cm-2 s-1 (10x the value reported in Weinberger et al. 2020). The distribution traces an asymmetric bi-lobe structure: ⅓ of the mass is moving away from the observer at 2000 km/s and thus redshifted. ⅔ of the mass is moving toward the observer at 1000 km/s and thus blueshifted. Each clump is broadened by 1000 km/s or 3.85 keV (1-sigma).
