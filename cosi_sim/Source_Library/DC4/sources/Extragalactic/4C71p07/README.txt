This folder contains the spectral model of 4C +71.07.

The spectrum is taken from Model A of Tavecchio et al. 2025 (https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2025A%26A...694L...3T/abstract).

The goals for these data are
1. Fit the spectrum with a log-parabola and determine the total flux in the COSI band
2. Measure the polarization fraction and angle of the source
