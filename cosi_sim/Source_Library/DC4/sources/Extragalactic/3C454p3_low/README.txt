This folder contains the spectral model of 3C 454.3 in the low state.

The spectrum is taken from the low state model of Bonnoli et al. 2011 (https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2011MNRAS.410..368B/abstract).

The goals for these data are
1. Derive the light curve and identify the flare
2. Measure the polarization fraction and angle of the source during the flare
